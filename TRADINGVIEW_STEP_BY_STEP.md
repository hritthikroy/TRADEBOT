- Open https://www.tradingview.com/chart/
- Click "Trading Panel"
- Select "Paper Trading"
- Search "BTCUSDT"
- Set timeframe to "15m"

### 3. Place Order:
- Click "BUY"
- Price: 91420.50
- Stop Loss: 91100.00
- Take Profit: 92500.00
- Amount: 0.00014 BTC
- Click "Place Buy Order"

### 4. Wait:
- Trade will close automatically at TP or SL
- Check your profit/loss
