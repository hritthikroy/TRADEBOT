## üî¨ KEY OPTIMIZATION INSIGHTS

### 1. Lower Confluence = More Trades
- Optimal confluence level: 4-5 (not 6-8)
- More signals without sacrificing quality
- Better trade frequency

### 2. Tighter Stops = Better Risk/Reward
- 0.5-1.5 ATR stops work best
- Allows for larger position sizes
- Reduces drawdown

### 3. Wider Targets = Higher Returns
- TP1: 2-5 ATR
- TP2: 3-7.5 ATR
- TP3: 5-12.5 ATR
- Let winners run!

### 4. Risk Management Sweet Spot
- 1-2.5% risk per trade
- 2% is optimal for most strategies
- Scale based on confidence
